📋 How to Use This Tag:

This is what your garment tag will look like when printed. It's designed for 60mm thermal label printers (compatible with Black Copper and similar brands).

  • Open this file: Right-click → "Open in Browser" or drag into Chrome/Edge
  • Print test: Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac)
