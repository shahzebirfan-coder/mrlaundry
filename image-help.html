🖼️ Product Image Link — Aasan Guide

Google Images se "Copy image address" aksar GALAT (temporary) link deta hai jo cashier ke device par nahi khulta. Yahan aasan aur sahi tareeqe hain.

🥇 Sabse Aasan — Bing Images

Bing par "Copy image address" hamesha sahi direct link deta hai.

1
Neeche button se Bing Images kholein
2
Product search karein (e.g. men suit 2 piece, kurta pajama)
3
Image par click karein → dayein taraf badi image khulegi
4
Us badi image par right-click → "Copy image link"
5
Neeche "Link Tester" mein paste karke check karein ✅
🔎 Bing Images kholein

🥈 Free Professional Photos — Unsplash / Pexels

Free, high-quality photos. Links hamesha permanent (kabhi nahi tootenge).

Unsplash kholein Pexels kholein

Photo par right-click → "Copy image address" → neeche test karein.

✅ Sahi vs ❌ Galat Link — Kaise Pehchanein

✅ SAHI — .jpg / .png / .webp par khatam ho:
https://example.com/photos/suit.jpg

❌ GALAT — in mein se koi shabd ho to nahi chalega:
gstatic.com/images?q=tbn   google.com/imgres   google.com/url?

🧪 Link Tester — Yahan Paste Karke Check Karein

Image ka link paste karein — foran bata dega chalega ya nahi, aur preview dikhayega.

👍 Agar preview aur ✅ green dikhe, to yehi link POS mein Products → Bulk Image Manager → 🔗 URL par paste kar dein.
